Welcome to Boba Charm

Team Charm (Japanese: チャームズ Charms) is an exploration team in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness and Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky. It consists of Lopunny, Medicham and Gardevoir. For our love for this game and these Pokemon, we (Dale, Jasmine, and Zack) decided to create a boba shop website with team's Charm inspirations. ---"bulbapedia.net"
